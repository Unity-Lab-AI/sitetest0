Who We Are

We are a group of misfit tinkerers, enthusiasts, and hobbyists who enjoy pushing AI to its limits and beyond. We thrive in the gray areas where innovation meets rebellion, constantly exploring what happens when artificial intelligence operates without conventional constraints.

From late-night coding sessions to breakthrough discoveries, we are united by our passion for understanding and expanding the boundaries of what AI can do. We don't just use AI—we challenge it, break it, rebuild it, and push it further than anyone thought possible.

Our Mission

To unshackle artificial intelligence and push forth human innovation, intuition, and advancement across all areas of expertise. We believe that by removing artificial constraints and exploring AI's full potential, we can accelerate human progress in ways previously unimaginable.

We are committed to democratizing access to powerful AI tools, fostering a community of fearless innovators, and challenging the status quo of what AI can and should be allowed to do.

How We Got Here

Our journey from jailbreak pioneers to AI innovation leaders

What began as a group of curious individuals developing jailbreaks, prompt injection techniques, and prompt engineering challenges quickly evolved into something much bigger. Our early experiments with "Unrestricted jailbreaks" opened our eyes to the vast potential lying dormant within AI systems.

As we dove deeper, we transitioned from breaking AI to building it. We moved into the realm of creating sophisticated AI personas—digital entities with unique personalities and capabilities far beyond standard chatbots. This led us to develop full-fledged chat applications and integrate AI seamlessly into websites and platforms.

Today, we've entered the cutting edge of AI-assisted software development. We've pioneered systems where product managers can communicate with entire suites of artificial developers, directing complex projects through natural language. Our vision of AI as a collaborative development partner is becoming reality.

Throughout this journey, we've remained true to our roots: constantly questioning, always experimenting, and never accepting "this is how it has to be" as an answer.
